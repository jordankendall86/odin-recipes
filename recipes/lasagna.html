Lasagna

An easy to make lasagna recipe with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
    • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
    • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
    • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ¼ tsp pepper
  • Meat Sauce
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
    • ¾ lb. ground beef
    • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • ½ cup chicken broth
    • 40 oz. marinara sauce
    • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
    • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Lasagna
    • 12 lasagna noodles, plus extra in case of breakage
    • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Mix cheese filling.
  2. Mix meat sauce
  3. Spoon meat sauce on the bottom of a lightly greased casserole dish.
  4. Add 4 boiled lasagna noodles (or use no-boil or fresh noodles).
  5. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture on top.
  6. Add 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 two more times.
  8. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  9. Cover and bake at 375° for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Broil at the end if desired.
  11. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
