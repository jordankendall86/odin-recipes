Yellow Curry

Easy Thai Yellow Curry made with chicken and vegetables and served over steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Curry paste
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables (onion, carrots and potatoes)
  • Coconut milk
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Cornstarch
  • Fish sauce, lime juice and brown sugar
  • Hot cooked rice

Steps

  1. Cook onions. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium low heat. Add the onion and saute for a few minutes until softened.
  2. Add vegetables. Add the carrots, potatoes, ginger, garlic and stir well.
  3. Add curry paste. Saute for 3 minutes.
  4. Add coconut milk. Add 1 ½ cans of coconut milk to the pot. Stir cornstarch into remaining ½ can of coconut milk and add to the pot.
  5. Boil then simmer. Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes or until the chicken and potatoes are cooked through. Adjust the thickness of the curry by adding more water to thin, if needed.
  6. Add flavor. Stir in the fish sauce, brown sugar and lime juice. Simmer 5 more minutes.
  7. Serve over rice, zoodles, or even. spaghetti squash!
